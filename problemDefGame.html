Problem Game

Rules:
  1. When told by Dr. Lawrence refresh this page
  2. Working with your table group discuss the two items
  3. Develop a clear, one-sentence problem definition phrased as "How might we..." whose solution would encompass both items
  4. ie. if your items are "blankets" and "toothpaste" one example question could be "How might we design low cost items with greatest impact to the homeless population of Toronto?"
  5. If your table group has developed a good answer put up a hand to share, first answer the class agrees on wins!
